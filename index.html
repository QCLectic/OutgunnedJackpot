This dice roller is designed to be used with Two Little Mice games including: Household Outgunned, etc.

Dice Roller Rules:

  • Combine any attribute with any skill to get the number of dice to roll. Minimum: 2 Maximum: 9
  • Select the number of dice using the slider
  • Click Roll to roll the dice.
  • Level of Success
    • No matches = Failure
    • 2 numbers match= Basic Success.
    • 3 numbers match= Critical Success
    • 4 numbers match= Extreme Success
    • 5 numbers match= Impossible Success
    • 6+ numbers match= Jackpot!
  • After rolling and having at least one success, you can pick a number to keep and re-roll the rest
  • On a re-roll, If you do not have more matches then you will go down a level of success.
  • After re-rolling, you can "Go All In", meaning you can pick a number to keep and re-roll the rest. if you do not get more matches of your chosen number, you will fail.
  • Click, Reset to start all over.
